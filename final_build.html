Artefact

When the final prints would have been completed, they would have been combined using pins. This would have then had the electronics and mechanism in the exoskeleton as to keep it separate from the soap to reduce the risk of an electrical fire. It is important to note that both Resin and Silicon were considered as finishing techniques for the artefact. Both would have been suitable options as both would have created a waterproof seal for the artefact. However, if the original material of PP was used instead of PLA then these properties would be accounted for anyway and it would remove a step from the production process, this not only saves time but money as well. (Resin Pro, n.d.)
