Introduction

Project Background

Themed entertainment, often relies on theming to tell a story. Disney Tokyo, as an example, have hand wash stations around the park that are themed to each area. Disney is typically known for going to extreme measures to ensure that theming isn’t ruined. For instance, the creation of blending blue and go away green which were designed by Disney to hide, large buildings, trash cans, lighting and anything that could negatively impact the theming of an area. (FieldPulse, 2024; Tokyo Disney Resort, n.d.)

Aim

The aim of this project was to develop an automatic soap dispenser that had an alien theming. It’s also important to note that it would be commercially available outside of the target audience.

Objectives

The objectives of this project were to design and build an alien themed soap dispenser. This could be implanted in bathrooms that have a horror theming, the main target audience would be madam Tussauds however, it would be commercially available for other companies to purchase. The design phase would include creating multiple 2D sketches to provide the group with a base understanding of what the idea was going to be. This would then be followed by the creation of prototypes which could be adjusted as necessary. Finally, the completed design would be built and created. Some Prototypes were completed as CAD models, and some were built out of cheap materials.

With dark rides and themed rides becoming a fan favourite they are a great time to relax and enjoy. However, log flumes and drop towers are still rides that are personally deamed as scary.
