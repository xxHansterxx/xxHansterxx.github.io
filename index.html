About Me

About Me

A 21 year old student living in Stoke-On-Trent, currently studying AI and Robotics at the University of Staffordshire. Interests lie within theme parks, Inline Hockey, Skiing and hiking and also watching American Football supporting Minnesota Vikings.Lives with an energetic Cockapoo named Rosie, who shares the love for hiking and outdoor activities. Experience in retail since the age of 17 has helped develop a strong set of professional and interpersonal skills.

Hockey

At 11 years old Ice Hockey became a hobby, but at 12 years this changed to Inline Hockey playing for a team called Macclesfield Warriors. Training with both the senior and junior team for 4 years was enjoyable, but expanding to further teams at this point, even attending Great British trials allowed for the development of skill as well as friendships. Currently, attending yearly international competitions and taking part in the league is the most important.

Skiing

Skiing was always a large tradition within the family, so it was normal to ski frequently. By the age of 6, nothing was too difficult to ski and the family attended yearly trips to France. Despite not going as often, little else has changed withing Adult life.

Hiking

Hiking was another family pass time, many weekends were spent walking across the peak district. When becoming an Adult interest only grew more, with climbing both Snowdon and Kinder acout twice. This has also been an excuse top start nature photography.

Theme parks

Theme Parks

Fear of roller caosters comes naturally to most people. This was the case until the age of 13, when Stealth at Thorpe Park broke this fear. However, when growing older it was interseting to discover that roller coasters are not where the most joy can be found. With dark rides and themed rides becoming a fan favourite they are a great time to relax and enjoy. However, log flumes and drop towers are still rides that are personally deamed as scary.
